Orange Dacha is just outside of Pererenan-Canggu popular expat enclave, South-West of Bali about 40-90 minutes drive (depending on traffic) from the Ngurah Rai International Airport.

Pererenan, Echo and Batu Bolong beaches are easy 7-15 min drive away. Pepito Market grocery - 3 min down the road. Tanah Lot temple - 20 min.

Jalan Griya Mas number 62 Tumbak Bayuh - Pererenan
