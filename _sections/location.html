Our villa is situated next to the popular expat enclave Canggu, South-West Bali about 40-90 minutes drive (depending on traffic) from the Denpasar international airport.

Tucked away in serene Tumbak Bayuh village, surrounded by lush rice paddies and only ~3km away from world famous Echo Beach Villa Orange Dacha can be ideal accommodation for single surfers, getaway couples and families alike.
