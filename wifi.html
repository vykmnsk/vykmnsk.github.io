WiFi/Internet speed here is 10 Мбит/с which is generally quick enough to stream a 1080p (high-def) video or "Work From Home".

We have had several guests connecting back to their office in home country and working for weeks.

To compare - average Australian ADSL speed is just 8Mbps.
